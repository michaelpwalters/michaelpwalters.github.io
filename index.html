What I Do

Michael is a Senior Financial Operations Analyst, currently working for Andersen in their Chicago office, who contributes to the decision making of senior management and is transitioning into a more strategic leadership role by becoming a CMA. A foundational attribute of his is lifelong learning both professionally and in his personal life. Once adverse to the thought of failing, he now knows taking calculated risks and growing through failure will deliver many opportunities for continued growth. Michael enjoys developing others and is often thanked for his patience. He believes in the traditional mentor/mentee relationship but also strongly believes it is necessary to have mentors who are younger than himself.

He has made great contributions within Chicago and the Central Region at Andersen by self-learning and implementing technology and analytics. By becoming familiar with SQL, he has in one circumstance improved efficiency more than 90%.
